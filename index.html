Welcome to VIPS-TC
It was in the year 1997, that Dr. S.C. Vats, founder of Vivekananda Institute of Professional Studies-Technical Campus, met Swami Jitatmanandaji an eminent educationist management expert author of books on Modern Physics & Vedanta and Indian Ethos in Management, an active social worker and the editor of Prabudh Bharat(Awakened India), a journal started by Swami Vivekananda. As the destiny ordained under a silver oak tree at ‘Advait Ashram’ in Mayawati in Pithoragrah District of Uttaranchal, vibrating thoughts were flowing from Swamiji’s lips in the balmy aroma of the dense pine and fir forest and he almost gave a diktat to Dr. Vats to set up an Institute, for higher learning in the capital city of India, in the name of Swami Vivekananda, a prophet and a path finder.
Eminent Speakers at VIPS
On the auspicious occasion of the 131st anniversary of Swami Vivekananda's historic speech delivered in Chicago on September 11th, VIPS had the honor of hosting visionary technocrat and Co-Founder of HCL, Padma Bhushan Dr. Ajai Chowdhry, along with distinguished guest Dr. Avinash Chandra Pandey. Dr. Chowdhry, currently leading India’s Quantum Mission, inspired our students and faculty to actively contribute towards the realization of our Prime Minister’s vision of a 'Viksit Bharat'. His words of wisdom ignited a sense of responsibility and ambition, motivating us all to strive for a brighter, self-reliant India.
